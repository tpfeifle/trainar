Getting started
  1. Allow the site to access your camera and microphone
  2. Print out the marker. Alternatively you can try downloading it to your phone.
  3. Select your preferred animation/workout from the lefthand side
  4. Show the marker to the camera
  5. Press the pause button in the upper right, when the animation is at the right position. (alternatively say "STOP")
  6. Optional: Scale/Rotate the figure by using the sliders in the upper right.
  7. Enjoy!
